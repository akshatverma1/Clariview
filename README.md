# Clariview
Clariview addresses this issue by automatically summarizing product reviews into concise 2-3 line summaries. Our system utilizes advanced AI algorithms to analyze all available reviews for a given product, extracting key positive and negative points. The result is a brief, yet comprehensive, summary that allows users to quickly grasp the overall sentiment and key insights without having to read each review individually. 
What is Problem..?
With the overwhelming number of product reviews available online, customers find it challenging to read through each one to make informed purchasing decisions. The sheer volume of reviews makes it impractical and time-consuming to sift through them all.
1) 75% OF Consumers are influenced By Product Reviews When Making Purchase Online.
2) Trying to sort through hundreds of product reviews online can be overwhelming for consumers, making it difficult for them to quickly determine the overall sentiment and Purchase.
As a result, decision-making is hampered and online shopping is less efficient.
